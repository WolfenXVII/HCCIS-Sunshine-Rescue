Adoption Requirements

  • You must be an adult (18+) to adopt one of our rescues.
  • You must pay the adoption fee (listed on the adoption page of the rescue).
  • You must be financially stable enough to care for the rescue.
  • You must attend a supervised "meet up" session with the rescue you plan on adopting.

Please remember that we have to right to refuse adoption of a rescue if one or more of the Adoption Requirements are not met. We also have to right to refuse adoption if we have reason to believe that the rescue would be unhappy or not properly cared for in their new home. In order to officially finalize an adoption you will be required to complete and sign our checklist (this will be given to you after the meet up session).

Medical Information

  • All of our rescues have their shots and vaccinations up to date.
  • All of our rescues are neutered or spayed.
  • All of our rescues are microchipped.

Note that if a specific rescue has a medical condition it will be listed on their adoption page.

Adoption Costs

The adoption fee for Dogs is $500. The adoption fee for Cats is $250.

The adoption fee includes a certificate of proof of adoption.
